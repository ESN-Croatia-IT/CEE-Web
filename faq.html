Frequently Asked Questions

Who can attend the Croatian Erasmus Event?

This event is primarily for Erasmus students in Croatia, but anyone interested can buy a ticket and join the experience!

What is included in the Base Package?

The Base Package covers 3 nights of accommodation at Noa Glamping Resort, all meals (breakfast, lunch, and dinner), access to parties at Noa Beach Club, daily activities (like yoga, dance classes, workshops), and a day trip to Lun's olive garden.

How much does it cost?

The Base Package costs between €200-€230 per person, depending on the villa type. Optional packages, like the Party Package (€33) and the Sport Package (€33), can be added for extra fun.

How can I make my experience even better?
  • Party Boat Package: Throw on your bikini, sunglasses and sunscreen because this floating party is packed with a summer vibes. You'll be enjoying an open bar, foam party and swim stop, all while cruising the beautiful Adriatic Sea.
  • Sports Adrenaline Package: Grab your swimsuit and get ready for an adrenaline rush with your choice of a 3-hour wakeboarding session or two wild banana boat rides on the sparkling Adriatic.
How can I buy a ticket?

To buy a ticket, check our official Instagram page for detailed information and booking instructions.
Croatian Erasmus Event Instagram or on this link: https://www.noa-zrce.com/en/events/croatian-erasmus-event-2025

How do I get to the event?
  • Some ESN sections will provide transport. Keep an eye for updates in our social media groups for more details.
  • If you're traveling by car, Noa Glamping resort offers free parking.
What can I expect in terms of meals?

All meals are served buffet-style, offering a variety of options to suit different tastes and dietary needs.

Can I bring my own drinks and snacks?

Yes, you can bring your own drinks and snacks to the villas, but they are not allowed in the club space.

Is there a store nearby?

There are no stores near the resort (the closest one is a few kilometers away), so it's best to bring everything you might need with you. The resort has a tobacco shop, tattoo spot and barber shop on-site.

Which are the available payment methods?

Event tickets can be purchased only by card. At the resort you can pay for everything with both cash and card.

What if it rains during the event?

No worries! The Noa Beach Club is an open space but has a covered area to keep the party going, rain or shine.

Is there parking available?

Yes, there is free parking available at the resort.

Where can I find more information and updates?

Follow our official Instagram page for updates, booking info, and more!
