What are we offering?
Location

Nestled on Croatia's Island of Pag, Noa Glamping Resort offers eco-friendly villas with private pools and stunning Adriatic views. Just steps away, the world-famous Noa Beach Club hosts top DJs and unforgettable parties under the stars. It's the perfect blend of relaxation and thrilling nightlife!

Accomodation

Stay in modern, well-equipped villas at Noa Glamping Resort, each with a private terrace and mini pool. With space for 2 to 6 people, you'll have a comfortable spot to relax and quick access to the resort's top-notch amenities.

Transport

Some ESN sections will organize group transport, and you'll be kept informed. If you're driving, there's free parking. Flying in? Zadar Airport is the nearest, with local connections available.

Food

All meals — breakfast, lunch, and dinner — are included, served at the resort's restaurant. You can bring your own snacks and drinks for your villa, but not inside the club area.
